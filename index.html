Frequently Asked Questions

Is Begoo free?

Yes! Begoo is free with unlimited transcriptions and no daily limits.

Is my data private?

100% private. All transcription happens locally on your Mac. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud.

Can I use Begoo for commercial purposes?

Free for individuals and small teams. For businesses with 10+ employees, please contact us at sales@getbegoo.com for commercial licensing.

How do you make money?

We operate on an honor system with commercial licensing for businesses with 10+ employees. This sustainable model allows us to keep core transcription free for everyone.

Do I need an internet connection?

No! Begoo works completely offline. All transcription happens locally on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Begoo free?

Yes! Begoo is completely free for everyone—individuals, startups, and businesses of any size. Unlimited transcriptions, no daily limits, no restrictions.

Is my data private?

100% private. All transcription happens locally on your Mac. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud.

Can I use Begoo for commercial purposes?

Absolutely! Begoo is free for commercial use with no restrictions. We're building optional enterprise features (AI integrations, team tools) for those who need more power.

Do I need an internet connection?

No! Begoo works completely offline. All transcription happens locally on your device.

How accurate is the transcription?

Begoo uses state-of-the-art AI models (including Whisper) for high-accuracy transcription. Accuracy depends on audio quality, accent, and background noise, but most users find it highly accurate for everyday use.

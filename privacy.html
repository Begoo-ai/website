Privacy Policy for Begoo

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

TL;DR: Your voice stays on your device. All transcription happens locally. Crash reporting is optional and opt-in only. No accounts, no tracking, 100% free.

What We Collect

Your Audio & Transcriptions

Audio is processed 100% locally on your Mac. It's never stored permanently or uploaded anywhere. Transcription text stays on your device only.

Optional Crash Reporting (Opt-In)

Crash reporting is OFF by default. You must explicitly enable it in Settings → Privacy.

If you choose to enable crash reporting, we use Sentry to collect:

  • Anonymous crash reports and error logs
  • App performance metrics
  • Device type and macOS version
  • Never collected: Your transcription text, audio data, or any personal information

Privacy Filters: Even if crash reporting is enabled, all transcription text is automatically redacted from error reports.

Learn more: Sentry Privacy Policy

Transcription Engines

Whisper.cpp

Runs entirely on your device. No data leaves your Mac.

Apple Speech Recognition

Also runs entirely on your device. We use on-device recognition only—no audio is sent to Apple's servers.

Permissions

Begoo needs:

  • Microphone: To record your voice
  • Accessibility: To type transcribed text into other apps
  • Speech Recognition: To use Apple's on-device speech recognition (optional)

Your Control

  • All settings and transcription history are stored locally on your Mac
  • Crash reporting is opt-in and can be disabled anytime in Settings → Privacy
  • Uninstall the app to delete everything
  • No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking

Data Storage

Begoo stores data only in your Mac's local storage:

  • Transcription History: Stored in SwiftData (local database)
  • Settings: Stored in UserDefaults (local preferences)
  • Whisper Models: Downloaded to ~/Library/Application Support/Begoo/models/
  • Temporary Audio: Automatically deleted after transcription
